== Description ==

Introducing our new WordPress plugin, the ultimate solution for effortlessly creating, organizing, and managing Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) on your website. Whether you're a small business, a large corporation, or an individual blogger, this plugin is designed to streamline the process of providing clear, concise, and accessible information to your visitors. With an intuitive interface and robust features, managing your FAQs has never been easier.

Key Features:

User-Friendly Interface:
Our plugin offers a clean and intuitive interface that allows you to add, edit, and organize FAQs with ease. No coding skills required! The drag-and-drop functionality ensures you can reorder questions and categories effortlessly.

Customizable FAQ Layouts:
Choose from multiple layout options to display your FAQs. Whether you prefer an accordion style, a simple list, or a categorized grid, our plugin provides flexible display settings to match your website's design.

Accordion and Toggle Effects:
Enhance user experience with dynamic accordion and toggle effects. Visitors can easily expand and collapse questions to find the information they need without cluttering the page.

Search and Filter Functionality:
Improve navigation with a built-in search bar and filter options. Users can quickly find answers to their queries by typing keywords or filtering by categories.

Responsive and Mobile-Friendly:
Our plugin is fully responsive, ensuring your FAQs look great on all devices, from desktops to smartphones. Your users will have a seamless experience regardless of their device.

Customizable Design:
Tailor the appearance of your FAQs to match your brand. Customize colors, fonts, and styles through the WordPress Customizer or use custom CSS for advanced styling.

Rich Content Support:
Add multimedia elements to your FAQs, including images, videos, and links. Enrich your answers with detailed explanations, tutorials, and visual aids to enhance user understanding.

Shortcode Integration:
Easily embed FAQs anywhere on your site using shortcodes. Place them within posts, pages, or widget areas to provide contextual help where it's most needed.

Multi-Language Support:
Reach a global audience with multilingual support. Our plugin is compatible with popular translation plugins, allowing you to provide FAQs in multiple languages.

SEO-Friendly:
Boost your search engine rankings with SEO-friendly FAQ schema markup. This helps search engines understand your content better and increases the likelihood of appearing in rich results.

Analytics and Insights:
Gain insights into your FAQs' performance with built-in analytics. Track views, search queries, and user interactions to identify popular questions and improve your content.

Import/Export FAQs:
Easily migrate your FAQs from one site to another with the import/export feature. Save time and ensure consistency across multiple sites.

Regular Updates and Support:
Enjoy peace of mind with regular updates and dedicated support. Our team is committed to providing you with the latest features and prompt assistance whenever you need it.

Why Choose Our Plugin?
Creating and managing FAQs is essential for improving user experience, reducing support queries, and enhancing customer satisfaction. Our plugin is designed with both beginners and advanced users in mind, offering a comprehensive solution that is easy to use yet powerful enough to meet complex needs. Whether you're providing product information, addressing common concerns, or offering detailed tutorials, our FAQ plugin is your go-to tool for efficient and effective communication.

Get Started Today:
Transform the way you handle FAQs on your WordPress site. Download our plugin now and start creating a more informative and engaging experience for your visitors. With our plugin, you can ensure that your audience always finds the answers they need, quickly and easily.

== Installation ==

= From your WordPress dashboard =

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
2. Search for 'EWD FAQ'
3. Activate the plugin from your Plugins page.
4. Go to the 'FAQs' menu in your WordPress dashboard.
5. Add new FAQs by clicking 'Add New FAQ'.

= From WordPress.org =

1. Download 'EWD FAQ'.
2. Upload the 'EWD FAQ' directory to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...)
3. Activate 'EWD FAQ' from your Plugins page.
4. Go to the 'FAQs' menu in your WordPress dashboard.
5. Add new FAQs by clicking 'Add New FAQ'.
